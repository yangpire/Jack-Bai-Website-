About Me

Urban Design and Landscape Architecture

My name is Jack Bai. Currently, I study Urban Design at Columbia University in the city of New York. I also completed my studies with a masters degree in Landscape Architecture and acquired my LEED Green Associate in 2014. There, I had the opportunity to study design overseas in Jordan and Finland where I worked with local design professionals and local students on sustainable urban development design principles. My travel and study abroad experiences transformed my life and provided me another opportunity to analyze how social infrastructures, such as Scandinavian, Asian, Middle Eastern and American, to operate in the city.
